Recycling Point – Latouros & Xenis Bros Ltd

The purpose of the recycling plant is to sort the mixed C&D waste into different components of waste such as concrete, asphalt, wood, metals, paper, plastic, etc, and process the sorted waster to produce clean recycled aggregates that can be used again in the construction industry.

Veltia Cyprus Ltd

Our Quote
For over 45 years, our products have helped improve the lives of in Cyprus. Our customers use our products to build the basic infrastructure that enables higher standards of living so that people have access to roads, bridges, hospitals, schools and so much more. We constantly try to maintain the high quality of our products while also preserving the environment, because, if something cannot be grown, it has to be mined responsibly.
